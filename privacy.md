# Privacy Policy

**Yendor** is a single-player offline game developed by Atypical Consulting.

## Data Collection

**Yendor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** The app runs entirely on your device.

Specifically, the app:

- Does **not** collect any personally identifiable information
- Does **not** use analytics or tracking services
- Does **not** use cookies or similar technologies
- Does **not** transmit any data over the network
- Does **not** require an internet connection to function
- Does **not** access your contacts, photos, location, or any other device data
- Does **not** contain any third-party advertising

## Local Data

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Game settings** (display preferences, key bindings) — stored in your browser's local storage
- **Save files** — stored in the app's local data directory
- **High scores** — stored in your browser's local storage

This data never leaves your device and is fully under your control. You can delete it at any time by removing the app or clearing its data.

## Children's Privacy

Yendor does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Third-Party Services

Yendor does not integrate with any third-party services that could collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at:
